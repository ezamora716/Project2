Stevie Nicks

Who is Stevie Nicks?

Stevie Nicks is a singer known for both her work in Fleetwood Mac and as a solo artist.

Why is she so special?

Stevie Nicks was the first woman to have been inducted twice into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame!
